Carports

It is the hope of anyone who buys a car or automobile to have it protected from the elements of weather. Our carports have you covered.Quality and strong steel poles are used for the structure.The top is covered by a material called shade net. Shade net just like automotive rexine is available in various colours.Green,Silver,Grey,Terracotta,Navy etc are some of the colours that we have in stock. The shade net is a special material of up-to from 40% to 90% finley and perfectly woven shade net.Shade net can also be used for green houses.

For the carports we provide a fix and supply service and also repair old ones that may be torn or simply need replacing to change the outside appearance of th place.
